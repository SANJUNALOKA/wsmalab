Web social media analysis is the process of collecting, monitoring, and interpreting data from social media platforms to gain insights into user behavior, public sentiment, trending topics, brand perception, and audience engagement. It involves analyzing metrics such as likes, shares, comments, and hashtags, as well as performing sentiment and trend analysis to support business decisions, marketing strategies, and research objectives. This analysis helps organizations understand their online presence, track competitors, identify influencers, and improve customer engagement.
